About Us

The Students of our Generation is facing more challenges towards their Employment in this Competitive Society. We a Team of Professionals Working in Multiple Domains Join hands together to create awareness to the students with their Post Employment after completing their Degree.

Creating awareness and Motivating Students towards Employment is the main vision of our Crew. We are conducting Employment career awareness program “E-CAP 2020” for 3rd Year & Final Year Students of Various prestigious institutions.
